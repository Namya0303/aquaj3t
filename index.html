Battery Life

AquaJ3t comes with a TYPE-C 3m charging cord and a nine watt adaptor.

The Aquaj3t, once fully charged can run upto 9 Hours without any interuptions. It has an automated warning viberation set 15 minutes before the battery runs out to prevent any accidents with our dear community which last for 5 minutes making it practically impossible to miss



Fitness Records

Aquaj3t Has a 8GB memory drive installed in it which records thee activity and automatically calculates the calories burnt by the user. It also records the usage time of previous three uses lasting more than 30 minutes



Dimensions

AquaJ3t is a compact hoverboard made to work on water.
It has the dimensions of 20inx5in which can fit in all small sized suitcases made by leading brands.



Remote Play

AquaJ3t is a device which records your usage stats which can be projected on your mobile screen. This mobile app helps you with your device and can help you configure the speed and lighting of the AquaJ3t. The photos captured by the 16 MegaPixel camera installed in the AquaJ3t are also visible here and can be saved and edited.

The app is available in All major app stores and at //https:namya0303.github.io/aJ3t1.o



Camera

AquaJ3t has a 16 MegaPixel Camera installed at both sides of the device: front and back. The camera automatically clicks pictures every 2 minutes while being used and it is upto you if you keep them or delete them forever: from your mobile and from your AquaJ3t.



Automated Balance System

AquaJ3t has an Automated Balance System which helps it to maintain its balance no matter how strong the waves are or how heavy the user is.
AquaJ3t gurentees that if used properly with precautions will not sink. The Automated Balane System has put Machine Learning to new heights where it has told the machine about X;Y;Z Axis and how to maintain them under any circumstances
